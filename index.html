Our Story

More Than Clothes — It's a Brotherhood

Brothers' Garments was born on the streets of Kampala with one mission: to bring quality, affordable fashion to every young man and woman who takes pride in how they dress. We started small, with a single rack of jackets at HAM Mall. Today we stock hundreds of styles.

Every piece we carry is handpicked — from bold bomber jackets to clean two-piece sets, fresh tracksuits to statement hats. We don't sell fast fashion. We sell pieces you'll wear for years.
